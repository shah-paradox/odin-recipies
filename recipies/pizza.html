Pizza Recipe

Description:

Pizza is a timeless and simple Italian delight, known for its minimalistic ingredients
and robust flavors. This pizza is a perfect example of how quality ingredients
can create a delicious meal without the need for complicated toppings or sauces.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil

Steps:

  1. Prepare pizza dough
    1. Mix flour, water, yeast and olive oil
    2. Knead the dough until smooth.
    3. Let it rise for 1-2 hours.

  2. Prepare Sauce:
    1. Blend canned tomatoes with garlic, olive oil, salt, and basil.

  3. Preheat Oven:
    1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature (usually 475-500°F or 250-260°C).

  4. Roll Out Dough:
    1. Roll out the risen dough on a floured surface to desired thickness.

  5. Assemble Pizza:
    1. Mix flour, water, yeast and olive oil
    2. Knead the dough until smooth.
    3. Let it rise for 1-2 hours.

  6. Bake Pizza:
    1. Transfer pizza to a preheated pizza stone or baking sheet.
    2. Bake for 10-15 minutes, until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

  7. Finish and Serve:
    1. Remove from the oven and drizzle with olive oil.
    2. Garnish with fresh basil.
    3. Slice and serve hot.
