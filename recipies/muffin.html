Muffin Recipe

Description:

A muffin is a small, cup-shaped baked good that is soft, moist, and slightly crumbly in texture.
It can be sweet or savory and often includes mix-ins like fruits, nuts, or chocolate chips.
Sweet muffins are a popular breakfast item and snack, enjoyed for their delicious flavor and versatility.
They are typically enjoyed warm, sometimes with a pat of butter or a drizzle of honey.

Ingredients:

  • Dry Ingredients:
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • 1 tablespoon baking powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Wet Ingredients:
    • 1 cup milk
    • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Optional Mix-ins:
    • 1 cup blueberries, chocolate chips, or nuts
    • Zest of 1 lemon or orange
    • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon or nutmeg

Steps:

  1. Preheat Oven:
    1. Preheat to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Line or grease a muffin tin.
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients:
    1. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
    2. Mix Wet Ingredients:
    3. Whisk together milk, oil, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Combine Ingredients:
    1. Stir wet ingredients into dry until just combined.
  4. Add Mix-ins:
    1. Gently fold in desired mix-ins.
  5. Fill Muffin Tin:
    1. Fill each cup 2/3 full with batter.
  6. Bake:
    1. Bake for 18-20 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Cool:
    1. Cool in the tin for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
  8. Serve:
    1. Enjoy warm or at room temperature.
